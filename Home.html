CUC_ParaConc

CUC_ParaConc is mainly used to retrieve bilingual and multilingual parallel corpus, supporting plain text corpus retrieval for Unicode, UTF8, ANSI and other encodings, and parallel corpus retrieval for multiple languages, such as Chinese, English, French, Russian, Korean, Japanese, Thai, etc. It can realize a 1-to-16 parallel corpus retrieve, i.e., translations from 1 to 16 countries aligned with one original text. The more widely used version at present is CUC_ParaConc V0.3.
