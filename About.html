CUC_ParaConc

ParaConc is a plain text corpus retrieval tool for retrieving bilingual or multilingual (multi-translation) corpora.

There are three widely used tools in the market today:
ParaConc developed by Dr. Michael Barlow
BFSU_ParaConc designed and developed by Prof. Jiajin Xu, Prof. Maocheng Liang and Yunlong Jia
CUC_ParaConc developed by Dr. Nanchang Cheng
The three tools have similar functions in retrieving corpus, and this website takes CUC_ParaConc as an example to introduce the its usage.
